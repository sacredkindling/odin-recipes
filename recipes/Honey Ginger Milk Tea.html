Honey Ginger Milk Tea

Honey ginger milk tea is a quick fix to cold and fever. This simple tea can make you refreshed and give you all the warmth that you deserve.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • Honey to taste
  • Tea sachet

Directions

  1. In a small saucepan, heat tea sachet and milk until scalded and steaming (small bubbles will appear along the sides of the pan).
  2. Add grated ginger to the hot milk and stir to incorporate. Turn off heat and let sit for 2 â€“ 5 minutes, thoroughly steeping the ginger in the milk. Reheat before pouring into a cup, if necessary.
  3. To serve, pour into a cup, straining the milk through a fine mesh strainer to remove the ginger. Use the back of a spoon to squeeze any remaining milk out of the grated ginger. Add honey to taste, stirring to dissolve completely.
